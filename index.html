About Us

Train With Lefty is a young and vibrant football academy that has establsished itself in the local football scene in New Dehi. Nurturing and identifying talent by the academy is a testament to the commitment and effort of the academy's athletes, staff, and coaches. We offer training sessions to individuals which take cares of cardio , strengthening and also helps in learning a new sport football.

Mission Statement

To provide a holistic and innovative training environment that fosters technical, tactical, physical, and mental growth, enabling aspiring players to reach their full potential on and off the field.

Vision

To be recognized as a leading football academy globally, known for producing technically proficient, intelligent, and resilient players who embody sportsmanship, teamwork, and a passion for excellence. We strive to empower our athletes with the skills, values, and opportunities needed to succeed at the highest level of the game while nurturing their personal growth and instilling a lifelong love for football.
