About Us

Train With Lefty is a young and vibrant football academy in South Delhi. Help people break free from the hustle bustle of corporate life with the fusion of football & fitness. A program to make contacts off your field while in the field. We offer training sessions to individuals which take cares of cardio, strengthening and also helps in learning a new sport football.

Mission Statement

To liberate individuals from the chaotic world of corporate life by blending the realms of football and fitness. Through our comprehensive training program, we aim to foster a sense of community and facilitate meaningful connections both on and off the field.

Vision

At our football academy, we envision a future where people can escape the demands of their corporate lives and find solace in the fusion of football and fitness. We strive to create an environment that not only provides exceptional training sessions, but also prioritizes cardiovascular health, strength development, and the acquisition of football skills. By offering a platform for personal growth and social interaction, we aspire to empower individuals to lead balanced, active lifestyles while expanding their networks within and beyond the field.
